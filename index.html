Hear the Orbit

An interactive sonification exhibit that transforms orbital motion into sound. Before entering the full interactive experience, you will compare two satellites whose motion looks almost the same, but sounds different.

This demonstration shows how sonification can reveal subtle changes that are difficult to notice visually.

Can You Hear the Difference?

These two satellites follow nearly identical paths. One satellite experiences a very small course correction. Visually, the difference is minimal. Sonically, the change is much easier to notice.

Compare the two, then continue to the main interactive orbit exhibit.
